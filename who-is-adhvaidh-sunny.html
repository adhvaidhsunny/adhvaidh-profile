Who is Adhvaidh Sunny?

Adhvaidh Sunny (listed as Adhvaidh S on NASA.gov) is an 18-year-old AI Systems Engineer and NASA Space Apps Global Winner. He won 1st Place worldwide at NASA Space Apps 2025 for "Best Use of Data", competing against 140,000+ participants globally. He has also won hackathons at OpenAI, UC Berkeley, and Princeton.

Adhvaidh Sunny is a software engineer and AI researcher based in the San Francisco Bay Area. At just 18 years old, he has already built a remarkable track record of wins at the world's most competitive hackathons.

His most notable achievement is winning NASA Space Apps 2025 as a Global Winner, where his project "Where is the Water?" earned 1st Place for "Best Use of Data" among 18,800+ teams from 150+ countries.

Adhvaidh currently works as an AI Systems Engineer at Riverlog Software , where he builds AI-powered logistics solutions.
